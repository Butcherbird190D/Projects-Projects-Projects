Description:
-------------
This Java program simulates the time it takes for a line at a bank to cycle from full to completely empty based on the time the bank closes (int), the amount of tellers (int), and the amount of queues (int).

Outputs:
--------------
A chat of average transaction times, wait times, and amount of served customers per teller. The final closing time is added at the end of the chart.
